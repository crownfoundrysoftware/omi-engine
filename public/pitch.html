Owner Move-In Notice Compliance Tool

120-Day Owner Move-In — Sacramento

Problem

Owner move-in notices are one of the most litigated eviction paths in Sacramento. Small errors in eligibility or timing can invalidate service and increase legal exposure.

What This Tool Does

  • Determines eligibility for a 120-day owner move-in notice
  • Calculates lawful service and termination dates
  • Produces a clear, reviewable compliance audit trail

What It Does Not Do

  • Does not replace legal counsel
  • Does not file or serve notices
  • Does not infer or guess factual inputs

Who It’s For

  • Sacramento property managers
  • Small landlord and housing attorneys
  • Owner-operators managing residential units

Pricing

  • Monthly subscription
  • No free tier
  • No per-notice fees

This tool generates compliance calculations based on provided inputs and current Sacramento and California law. It does not constitute legal advice. Verify facts before service.
